About us

Sobhy Saeed Ceramic and Porcelain Company is one of the leading companies in the production and manufacturing of high-quality ceramics and porcelain. The company was founded in 1990 by Engineer Sobhy Saeed, who had a new and ambitious vision to offer unique and innovative products in this field. Sobhy Saeed Company is known for its unique and elegant designs that cater to diverse customer needs. The company offers a wide range of products, including tiles, utensils, decorations, and other accessories made of ceramic and porcelain. The company relies on the latest technologies and equipment to ensure quality and precision at every stage of production. Sobhy Saeed Company is committed to meeting customer expectations and providing high-quality products that meet international standards. The company works with a team of professional engineers and technicians who strive to fulfill the founder's vision of delivering the best products and services. Sobhy Saeed Company has an excellent reputation in the market due to the quality of its products, adherence to deadlines, and fast delivery. The company is considered a trusted partner for many customers in both the local and international markets. In short, Sobhy Saeed Ceramic and Porcelain Company is a leading name in the ceramic and porcelain industry, deserving praise for its distinctive designs and product quality. The company continues to excel and succeed in this field while meeting customer needs.
